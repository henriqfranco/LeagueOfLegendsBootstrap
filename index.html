League of Legends

League of Legends, commonly referred to as League, is a 2009 multiplayer online battle arena video game developed and published by Riot Games. Inspired by Defense of the Ancients, a custom map for Warcraft III, Riot's founders sought to develop a stand-alone game in the same genre.

Features and Updates

League of Legends is updated every two weeks!

As such, the Riot Games team works tirelessly to periodically release new League of Legends patches every two weeks to make adjustments wherever needed. These patches often include bug fixes, game improvements, balance changes, and new content like new champions or skins.
